DashPay - Username payments without any hassle

Send funds to your friends using their username, privately. No more addresses or cryptographic knowledge required.
Easy
Payments with crypto never been easier: just press one button, and funds is already there. Instantly.
Private
Only two parties exchanging the funds can trace the transaction. No spectators. No traces to names.
Learn how it works
See the demo
  • Alice and Bob register usernames;
  • Alice creates a "Contact" document under the DashPay app on the Dash Platform. This document contains an extended public key encrypted by Alice for Bob's public key stored in his identity
  • Bob does the same. Now the connection between Alice and Bob is established;
  • If Alice wants to pay to Bob, she decrypts his extended public key encrypted for her, derives an address from it, and sends money there.
This way, both parties provided each other with public keys derived and encrypted specifically for this contact; Since it's an extended public key, both parties can generate a new address for every new payment, and that address will be unique for that connection. This makes those payments identifiable only by those two parties. It doesn't create any hassle for the users, as the whole process is abstracted away from the user's sight by the wallet. One user needs to discover the other user by their name in the wallet and press the "add to contacts" button, and after that, he can start sending the payments just with the press of the "send" button. And as an addition, those payments between users are absolutely untraceable.
